Our POV for Eastern

You're scaling distribution faster than your systems can keep up.

The divestiture of your extrusion business was the right move. But it shifted the complexity — from manufacturing overhead to supply chain coordination risk. With Oldcastle APG as your primary supplier and four newly acquired regional DCs to unify, the margin for manual error is shrinking as your geographic footprint grows.

⚠️ Where You Are Today

The "People Putty" Problem

  • ❌
    No formal sales forecasting. Seasonal inventory cycles are managed reactively — you're unwinding inventory manually instead of predicting demand.
  • ❌
    Excel-based data extraction. Pulling data out of Infor by hand introduces latency and human error into every inventory and procurement decision.
  • ❌
    Disconnected post-acquisition systems. Hudson Fence, Shoreline, Coastal Aluminum, and South Camden each brought different processes and data models. Coordination is expensive.
  • ❌
    Supplier coordination via email/phone. Your long-term supply agreement with Oldcastle APG is only as strong as the visibility and communication layer on top of it.
  • ❌
    IT bandwidth bottleneck. Your internal CRM is 85–95% built but stuck — every new automation requires IT tickets, not process-owner self-service.
✅ What Agentforce Operations Unlocks

An Intelligent Layer Over Infor

  • ✓
    AI-driven demand forecasting replaces Excel guesswork — seasonal patterns, regional variation, and contractor order history drive automatic reorder triggers.
  • ✓
    Real-time Infor ERP integration via native API connectors — no rip-and-replace. Agents read and write to Infor directly, surfacing status without manual extraction.
  • ✓
    Cross-DC inventory visibility across all regional distribution centers — unified view of PVC, aluminum, chain-link, and wood stock from one dashboard.
  • ✓
    Supplier collaboration agents send automated updates to Oldcastle APG via email — no new portals, no new logins. Your supplier just gets better information.
  • ✓

How It Works

Three moves. No rip-and-replace.

Agentforce Operations sits above your existing Infor ERP as an orchestration layer. Your data stays where it is. Your people gain time back.

1

Connect to Infor

Pre-built API connectors integrate with your Infor ERP in weeks — not months. Agentforce reads inventory levels, open POs, and supplier lead times in real time.

2

Deploy AI Agents

Autonomous agents handle the manual workflows your team currently runs by hand: procurement triggers, supplier status updates, inventory alerts, and demand signals.

3

Process Owners Take Control

Your supply chain and operations leads configure and tune automations through a no-code interface. No IT queue. No waiting. Changes live in hours, not weeks.
